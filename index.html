About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Economic Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur. My research interests are in Applied Microeconomics, with a focus on Health and Family Economics. I am also interested in the impact evaluation of public policies, gender, and sanitation economics.
